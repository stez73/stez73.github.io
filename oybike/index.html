Come and join our local cycling hub for weekly training rides and weekend adventures!

On Yer Bike! is a local cycling club for all ages and we arrange regular away days to experience some routes far from the familiar....and sometimes off the beaten track.

We have kids and seniors provision to cater for all abilities and experienced group leaders with a wealth of knowledge relating to cycling methods and maintenance.

Don't hesitate to call or drop in today and meet our friendly team!
